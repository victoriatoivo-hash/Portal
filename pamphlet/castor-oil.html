Castor Oil

From N$43.00

Cold Pressed Castor Oil is a thick, nutrient-rich vegetable oil extracted from the seeds of the castor plant (Ricinus communis) using a mechanical pressing method without heat. The cold pressing process helps preserve the oil’s natural nutrients, fatty acids, and beneficial compounds, resulting in a high-quality, potent oil ideal for skincare, haircare, and wellness applications.

Castor oil is especially rich in ricinoleic acid, a unique fatty acid known for its moisturizing, conditioning, and soothing properties. Its naturally thick texture creates a protective barrier that helps lock in moisture, making it particularly beneficial for dry skin, brittle hair, and weakened lashes or brows.

Cold pressed castor oil is widely used in beauty routines for strengthening hair, promoting scalp health, and deeply hydrating the skin.

Benefits

Hair and Scalp Support

Castor oil helps nourish the scalp, strengthen hair strands, and reduce breakage. Its thick coating action supports moisture retention and improves the appearance of fuller, healthier hair.

Deep Skin Hydration

It locks in moisture and protects the skin barrier, making it excellent for dry, cracked, or rough skin.

Brow and Lash Conditioning

Regular use may improve the appearance of thicker, healthier brows and lashes.

Muscle and Joint Comfort

Castor oil has traditionally been used to support relief from muscle tension and joint stiffness. The ricinoleic acid content is believed to contribute to its soothing properties when massaged into affected areas.

Inflammation Support

When applied topically, castor oil may help calm mild inflammation in the body. It is commonly used in castor oil packs placed over areas of discomfort to support circulation and relaxation.

Circulation Support

Massage with castor oil may help stimulate local blood flow, which can assist in relieving tension and promoting comfort in sore areas.

How to Use

Muscle and Joint Massage

Warm a small amount of castor oil in your hands and massage directly into sore muscles or stiff joints using firm, circular motions. Cover the area with a warm cloth for enhanced absorption. This method is often used after physical activity or at the end of the day to support relaxation.

Castor Oil Pack for Inflammation Support

Soak a clean cotton cloth in castor oil and place it over the desired area, such as the lower back, abdomen, or joints. Cover with plastic wrap and apply gentle heat using a warm water bottle for 20 to 30 minutes. This traditional method is commonly used to support circulation, ease tension, and promote overall body comfort.

Hair and Beauty Applications

Massage into the scalp and leave on for at least 30 minutes before washing. For skin hydration, apply a small amount to damp skin to seal in moisture.

Safety

Cold Pressed Castor Oil is for external use only unless directed by a qualified healthcare professional. Perform a patch test before first use to check for skin sensitivity.

When using heat packs, ensure the temperature is warm and not hot to avoid burns. Avoid applying to broken or irritated skin. If irritation occurs, discontinue use.

Pregnant individuals should consult a healthcare professional before using castor oil packs, especially on the abdominal area. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to preserve quality.
